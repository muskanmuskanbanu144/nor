Frequently Asked Questions

How do I renew my Norton subscription?

To renew your Norton subscription:

  • Open your Norton product and go to “My Subscriptions”.
  • Click “Renew” next to your product.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete payment.
Need help? Call us at 800‑828‑3670.

My Norton product isn't updating – what do I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
  • Open Norton and use the “LiveUpdate” feature.
  • Restart your system and disable other antivirus programs temporarily.
